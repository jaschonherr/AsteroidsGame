Asteroids!

Destroy all the asteroids! If they hit you, you will lose HP (top right). If that reaches zero, game over.
The ship will point towards your mouse
Press 'w' to accelerate in the direction the ship is facing
Press 'q' to enter hyperspace, teleporting you to a random location
Click to fire your twin cannons
Hold 'e' to fire grenade when Grenade Status = "Ready" and then let go to detonate
